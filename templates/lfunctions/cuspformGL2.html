Holomorphic cusp forms for GL(2)

Currently in the LMFDB, we have data on L-functions associated to holomorphic cusp forms with trivial character. These L-functions \(L(s) = \sum a_n n^{-s} \) have a {{ KNOWL("lfunction.functional_equation",title="functional equation")}} of the form \begin{equation} \Lambda_f(s) := N^{s/2} \Gamma_{\mathbb{C} } \left(s + \frac{k-1}{2} \right) L(s, f) \pm \Lambda_f(1-s), \end{equation} where \(a_n n^{\frac{k-1}{2} } \) is an algebraic integer.

In the plot below, L-functions are organized by the ordered pair \((N,k)\) where N is the {{ KNOWL("mf.elliptic.level",title="level")}} and k is the {{ KNOWL("mf.elliptic.weight",title="weight")}}. For a given \((N,k)\), the color indicates the sign of the functional equation, and the horizontal grouping indicates the degree of the field containing the arithmetically normalized coefficients. See the {{ KNOWL('lfunction.degree2holo.key', title='legend') }} for more details.
